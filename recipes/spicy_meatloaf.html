Spicy Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf with a kick! Add your favorite BBW sauce and some additional spices to give a simple classic a new twist!

This version of meatloaf takes the simple and easy to bake traditional recipe and let's you put your own tasty signature on the dish. Just add your favorite BBW sauce where the recipe instructs to add your own personal flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • Mixture: 1/2 cup ketchup and 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1.5 pounds lean ground beef
  • 0.75 pounds groun hot italian sausage
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In large bowl: mix beef/sausage with onion, garlic, salt, pepper, egg, and bread crumbs
  3. Add 3/4 of ketchup/BBQ sauce mixture to bowl and mix
  4. Spray loaf pan with non-stick cooking spray and form meat into pan
  5. Bake for 20 minutes
  6. Top with remainder of ketchup/BBW sauce mixture
  7. Bake additional 5-10 minutes until top just begins to brown
